# Data Availability

This publication repository does not include the actual study dataset.

Excluded files include raw Portable Health Clinic records, cleaned participant-level datasets, imputed datasets, merged analytical datasets, local exports, and any files containing potentially identifiable or row-level health information.

The included `tables/`, `figures/`, and `results/` folders contain derived aggregate outputs used for manuscript preparation.

Suggested manuscript wording to adapt:

> The participant-level data supporting this study are not publicly available because they contain health information collected through the Portable Health Clinic program and are subject to institutional, ethical, and data-sharing restrictions. The analysis code and aggregate manuscript outputs are available in this repository. Requests for access to the underlying data may be directed to the corresponding author and will be considered subject to applicable approvals and data-use agreements.
